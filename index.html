Info

Daybreak Metal Fest is an all-day festival hosted at The Castle Tavern in the heart of Luton. With nine UK bands from near and far, the lineup will cater to anyone with a taste for the extreme.


Travel

By Car

There are several car parks within walking distance, we recommend Vicarage Car Park which is free from 6pm.

By Train

The venue is an 8 minute walk from Luton train station. Trains run regularly, heading north to Bedford and south to London.
Check train times

By Bus

Buses run regularly from neigbouring towns. There are two bus stops within minutes walk from the venue.
